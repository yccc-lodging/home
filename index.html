Student Lodging

Welcome to the YCCC Student Lodging Site! Here you can find information about student lodging amenities, events, activities, and more! All information is provided by York County Community College Staff, Hampton Inn & Suites Staff, Residential Advisors, and community members. This is a community run website, for official YCCC information, please visit yccc.edu!

Events:

  • Birthday Parties for all participating residents (with the option for a pool party)
  • Study Sessions for midterm and finals weeks
  • Book Club every Monday and Wednesday night
  • Group Activities to improve students' mental and physical health
  • And more coming soon!
